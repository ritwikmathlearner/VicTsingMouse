    What is VicTsing?

    Found in 2012, VicTsing Technology Co., Ltd is a professional supplier of consumer electronic products and home appliance, especially specialized in the computer and car accessories and humidifier. Our mission is to provide innovative and practical products with competitive price so our clients can get access to high tech and have a simpler and better life.


    The mission motivates us to make progress continuously. Over the years, VicTsing has developed into one of the world’s favorite brand in the line of consumer electronics and home appliance. Boasting its annual sales of $75million, VicTsing’s retailers and distributors spread all over the world.


    In 2018, VicTsing’s sub-brand homasy has been built. Homasy is a brand about house appliences like oil diffuser, handheld vacuum etc. VicTsing diffuser will be branded with Homasy in the future.


    From 2019, VicTsing, our main brand, will focus on keyboard, mouse and office accessories.


    In the future, we will continue to adhere to our mission and provide creative products to meet our customers’ needs.
